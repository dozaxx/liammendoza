About Me

Welcome to my website, a portfolio I built using skills I've learnt in University and a Udemy Online Course. I am a Bachelor of Science graduate, with a double major in Information Systems and Computer Sciences from the University of Auckland. I am passionate about customer service, and offer an outlook on both the technical and business side of things, helping me strategise solutions to problems I am exposed to. Other projects that I have worked on include the Microsoft Student Accelerator and several University projects that deal with Analysis and Development.

Born in the Philippines, I came to New Zealand at the age of 8. Growing up, I was always a keen problem solver and loved to design and build things. As soon as I wrote my the first "Hello World" in Python, I instantly attracted to the idea of being able to make my ideas come to a reality. My interest for UX and UI came about because my love for design, and what better way to align this with my love for creating user interfaces. In the past 3 years, I have developed a broad range of skills through university and upskilling, ranging from Business Intelligence, to Networks, to Cyber Security. During my last semester, I was a marker and teaching assistant for papers covering Human-Computer Interaction (which teaches UX), and Software Fundamentals.

Hobbies and interests outside of my studies include Football, Fashion and Food. I am passionate about football. I have been playing for my boyhood club, Fencibles United AFC, and have so since our move to New Zealand in 08'. I currently play for the Mens First Team, and have previously captained our Mens Reserves and Under 19s teams. As well as playing, I offer my services to the club by coaching youth and junior players in holiday programmes.
